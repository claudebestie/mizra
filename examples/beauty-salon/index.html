Our Services

Haircut & Styling

Precision cuts and blowouts tailored to your face shape and lifestyle. From classic bobs to modern layers.

350-750 ₪

Manicure & Pedicure

Classic, gel, and art manicures plus relaxing pedicure treatments. Let your hands and feet shine.

מניקור קלאסי, ג'ל, וארט בשילוב עם טיפולי פדיקור מפנקים. לזרוח מכף רגל.

Facial Treatments

Deep cleansing, hydration, anti-aging, and glow facials. Customized to your unique skin needs.

Your dream wedding look, from trial to the big day. We create timeless bridal beauty that photographs perfectly.

600-1,200 ₪

Lash Extensions

Classic, volume, and hybrid lash sets. Wake up every morning with effortlessly gorgeous eyes.
