Commercial Law

Contract drafting, mergers and acquisitions, joint ventures, and comprehensive commercial dispute resolution for businesses of all sizes.

משפט מסחרי

Corporate Law

Company formation, shareholder agreements, corporate governance, regulatory compliance, and board advisory services.

דיני תאגידים

Litigation

Civil and commercial litigation, arbitration, mediation, and appellate advocacy across all Israeli courts and tribunals.

Employment Law

Startup & Tech

Venture capital transactions, intellectual property protection, technology licensing, ESOP plans, and startup legal frameworks.
