NOT JUST A
HAIRCUT.

Yossi's opened its doors on a quiet corner in Florentin with one chair, a straight razor, and a promise: every man who sits down leaves looking and feeling sharper than when he walked in.

This is the neighborhood barbershop done right. No rush. No small talk unless you want it. Just honest craft, cold beer, and a cut that holds up weeks later.
